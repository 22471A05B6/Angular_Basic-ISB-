About Our Tourism Web Portal

The Tourism Information and Booking Web Portal is a modern web-based platform developed to simplify travel planning and enhance the tourism experience.

Project Overview

This portal provides detailed information about tourist destinations, travel packages, hotels, and transportation options. Users can explore destinations, compare services, and make bookings through a single, user-friendly interface.

Objectives

  • To provide accurate and up-to-date tourism information
  • To simplify online booking for travel and accommodation
  • To promote tourism through a digital platform
  • To enhance user experience with an intuitive interface

Technology Used

The application is developed using Angular for the frontend, enabling component-based architecture, efficient routing, and a responsive user interface.
